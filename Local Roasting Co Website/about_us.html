ABOUT US

We're coffee people!

Local Roasting Co. was founded by owner James Hull in 2012. After extensive study, James began roasting beans that year and with help from friends was able to turn a piece of the old Film Exchange building in Northwest Portland into a cozy little spot to roast coffee and serve tasty beverages. Our doors at 1932 NW Lovejoy have been open six days a week since January 2013.


James

We roast our beans in small batches on our Diedrich IR-5 almost every day of the week, so all the coffee we serve in the shop is about as fresh as it can be. As with all crops, coffee beans are seasonal, so we offer the best coffee we can get our hands on throughout the year, and we try and purchase certified organic beans whenever possible. We also enjoy direct relationships with many of our suppliers, like Juan Atkins, whose family grows and imports our Juan's Organic Peruvian.
